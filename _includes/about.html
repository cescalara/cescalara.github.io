About me

I'm a PhD student at the KTH Royal Institute of Technology and Oskar Klein Centre in Stockholm, Sweden. I'm originally from the UK and have a degree in Physics from Imperial College London. Before moving to Stockholm, I completed a Master project in Plasma Physics for Nuclear Fusion at the École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL) in Switzerland and worked for a year at the European Space Agency in The Netherlands.
