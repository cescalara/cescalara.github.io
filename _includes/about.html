About me

I'm a postdoc at the Technische Universität München and ORIGINS Excellence Cluster in Munich, Germany. I'm originally from the UK and have a degree in Physics from Imperial College London. Before moving to Munich, I completed a Master project in Plasma Physics for Nuclear Fusion at the École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL) in Switzerland, worked for a year at the European Space Agency in The Netherlands and completed my PhD in astroparticle physics at the KTH Royal Institute of Technology in Sweden.
